# BRRRR Stress Test Calculator

See where a deal breaks before you buy.

**[Try it live](https://sbc1-code.github.io/deal-stress-tester/)**

## Why This Exists

Lessons from a real 4-plex: bought at $130K, rehabbed $108K, refi'd at $280K, sold at $400K, netted ~$15K over 4.5 years. No existing calculator would have shown that reality. Most calculators hide the ugly stuff. This one leads with it.

## What It Does

- Live cash flow, CoC return, and ROI calculations as you type
- Two stress test heatmaps: rate hikes vs. vacancy, rent decline vs. expense spikes
- BRRRR cycle analysis: rehab, ARV, refinance, cash-out proceeds (Pro)
- Exit analysis with projected proceeds, mortgage payoff, and partner splits
- Effective hourly rate estimate for your management time

## Privacy

Your numbers never leave your browser. No backend, no database, no analytics, no cookies.
